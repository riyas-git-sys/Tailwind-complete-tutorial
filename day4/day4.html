Day 4: Tailwind CSS Responsive Design & Components

Responsive Design

Breakpoints

Default (no prefix): Applies to all screen sizes

Example: text-center

sm: Applies to screens 640px and wider

Example: sm:text-left

md: Applies to screens 768px and wider

Example: md:flex

lg: Applies to screens 1024px and wider

Example: lg:grid-cols-3

xl: Applies to screens 1280px and wider

Example: xl:max-w-6xl

2xl: Applies to screens 1536px and wider

Example: 2xl:text-5xl
